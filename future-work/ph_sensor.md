# pH Sensor Integration

Status: Future Work

The current routing decision depends on TDS only. The primary document identifies pH sensing as a future improvement that could provide additional water-quality context.

## Motivation

TDS measures the amount of dissolved material, not the chemical identity or acidity/alkalinity of the water. Adding pH measurement would improve dashboard visibility and could support more informed warnings.

## Repository Honesty

No implemented pH sensor integration is claimed in the current laboratory prototype.
