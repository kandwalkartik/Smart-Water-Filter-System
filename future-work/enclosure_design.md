# Enclosure Design

Status: Future Work

The source document states that the current system is a breadboard prototype and has not been tested inside a real filter housing under household water pressure.

## Motivation

A practical purifier integration would require:

- Electrical isolation
- Moisture protection
- Mechanical mounting
- Valve and tubing support
- Service access
- Thermal and pressure considerations

## Repository Honesty

No production enclosure, certified housing, or field-ready purifier integration is claimed in this repository.
