# Energy Analysis

The primary document compares the smart-routing prototype against an always-RO baseline over the 100-hour laboratory trial.

## Note on Minor Difference

The source reports total energy consumed as 3.946 kWh in the 100-hour table and 3.938 kWh in the always-RO comparison table. This repository preserves both values in their original contexts rather than reconciling them.
