# Bill of Materials

The following bill of materials is based only on the primary project document.

| Item | Component | Specification | Qty | Purpose |
| ---: | --- | --- | ---: | --- |
| 1 | ESP8266 NodeMCU | v3, 80 MHz, 4 MB flash, built-in Wi-Fi | 1 | Microcontroller and Wi-Fi gateway |
| 2 | TDS sensor module | DFRobot Gravity TDS-10, 0-1000 ppm, analog 0-2.3 V | 1 | Water quality sensing |
| 3 | 2-channel relay module | 5V coil, 10A contact, optocoupler isolated | 1 | Valve switching |
| 4 | RO solenoid valve | 12V DC, normally closed, G1/4 thread | 1 | RO path control |
| 5 | UF solenoid valve | 12V DC, normally closed, G1/4 thread | 1 | UF path control |
| 6 | Green LED | 5 mm, 20 mA | 1 | Safety Mode indicator |
| 7 | Red LED | 5 mm, 20 mA | 1 | Saving Mode indicator |
| 8 | Push button | Momentary tactile switch | 1 | Mode selection |
| 9 | DC power supply | 12V 2A regulated adapter | 1 | System power |
| 10 | Current-limiting resistors | 330 ohm | 2 | LED protection |

## Cost Note

The source document states a target hardware cost of USD 15-20 for the complete system. This repository does not add supplier pricing or procurement claims beyond that statement.
